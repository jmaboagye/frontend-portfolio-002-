Qodesh-Phoenix

We love the Lord

As a denomination of the Lighthouse Group of churches, Qodesh Family Church-Phoenix, has its roots in Qodesh North and South America. We've been fellowshipping in Arizona since October 2012. We are a small but diverse group of Christians committed to the great commission of Jesus, sending missionaries and loving our community. QFC-Phoenix is theologically, culturally and socially diverse; we have leaerned to live with our differences by leveraging a diverse congregation in the beauty worship, study, fellowship and service. We welcome everyone, men and women , and children to serve and use their gifts in all aspects of the church's life and leadership. We are active in several campuses accross the Phoenix metropolis, including Firstlove Fellowship Tempe, FirstLove Fellowship Polytechnic Campus ans other cells scattered accrosss Mesa, Tempe, Gilbert, and Glendale communities. By adhering to tenets of Christian faith through the Holy scriptures, we uphold the Bible as our supreme source of authority and all members are encouraged to read and study the Bible (both as individuals and in study groups), that they may intepret scripture for themselves and apply it to their own lives. We meet on Sundays(10am-12:30pm) and Tuesdays(7pm-8pm) for our midweek services across the campuses and cells. You're welcome to join us to have a glorious fellowship with our Lord.
